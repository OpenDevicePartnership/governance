## Professional Background

Karan Dhillon is a Principal Product Manager in Microsoft's Windows System Silicon Integration (WSSI) team, where he leads the Open Device Partnership (ODP). With over 8 years of experience in devices teams at Microsoft and another 5 years in the device management, space Karan brings a committment to building secure, reliable and manageable devices.

As a Product Manager, Karan has a track recored of successfully delivering  projects with complex cross-organizational dependencies.

## Value Added to ODP

- Product Manager expertise in day to day running of the ODP organization.
- A proven track record of delivering specifications for features and scenarios.
- A deep understanding of customer scenarios and problems that need to be addressed in this space.

## Strategic Impact & Vision for ODP

Karan's contributions have been instrumental in the establishment of ODP alliance.

Key contributions to date:

- Drive alignment amongst various participating teams on the specific technologies included in ODP.
- Drive the implementation of the ODP website.
- Drive the documentation project to implement docs for ODP technologies.

Karan envisions ODP will enable OEMs to deliver secure devices with exceptional user-experience for scenarios currently delivered via UEFI Firmware.

## Commitments & Outcomes

These are the key outcomes I plan to focus on during this term:

- **ODP Governance**
  - Organization of the ODP steering committe: once a month steering committee meetings with clear agendas and outcome tracking.
    Success = Monthly meetings of the steering committee with agendas published in advance and tracked outcomes.
- **Minimal Firmware**
  - Work with various stake holders to identify attack surface reduction strategy for Patina Firmware.
    Success = A specification for delivering customer scenarios from a high level OS which are currently delivered from UEFI Firmware.
- **Broaden ODP Adoption**
  - Onboard more OEMs, IHVs and silicon providers into the ODP alliance.
    Success = At least two more OEMs and 1 more IHV is onboarded onto ODP.
